## 💪 About PrimeFit

PrimeFit is a fully offline fitness and diet planner designed to help users achieve their health and fitness goals anytime, anywhere.

### Key Features

- 🏋️ Personalized Workout Plans
- 📊 BMI Calculator with Instant Results
- 🥗 Diet & Nutrition Planning
- 📈 Progress Tracking
- 🎯 Daily Fitness Goals
- 🔒 Privacy-Focused (No Login Required)
- 🚫 No Ads, No Subscriptions
- 🌐 Fully Offline Functionality

### Why PrimeFit?

PrimeFit combines workout planning, nutrition guidance, BMI analysis, and progress tracking into a single lightweight application. The app is designed for students, professionals, fitness enthusiasts, and beginners who want a simple and effective fitness companion without relying on internet connectivity.
